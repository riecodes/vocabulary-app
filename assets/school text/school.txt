Teacher – A person who helps students learn.
Student – A person who goes to school to learn.
Classroom – A room where lessons take place.
Homework – Schoolwork that students do at home.
Book – A set of written pages used for learning.
Pencil – A tool used for writing or drawing.
Desk – A piece of furniture where students sit and work.
Blackboard – A board where teachers write with chalk.
Notebook – A book for writing notes.
Uniform – Clothes students wear to school.
Exam – A test to check what students have learned.
Subject – A specific area of study, like Math or Science.
Recess – A short break during the school day.
Library – A place in school with many books to read or borrow.
Backpack – A bag used to carry school supplies.
